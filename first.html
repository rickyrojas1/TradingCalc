Swing Trade Calculator

The purpose of this trade calculator is so you can easily enter the bitcoin value of a trade when you buy a cyptocurrency and then when you sell it. This will help you find your profits by comparing to bitcoin at real time prices and then calculating the profits or losses. The reason there are 6 spaces for each buy and sell is because sometimes trading platforms must split up your buy and sell orders into smaller orders. You do not need to enter all six values, if you only have one value just leave the rest blank it will work all the same. Happy Trading!
